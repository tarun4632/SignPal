About SIGNPAL

SIGNPAL is dedicated to bridging the communication gap for the deaf community through innovative technology solutions. Our mission is to provide accessible and effective tools to enhance communication and inclusivity for individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing.

Our team consists of experts in artificial intelligence, linguistics, and accessibility who are passionate about making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve. We believe in the power of technology to create a more inclusive world where everyone can communicate and connect with ease.
